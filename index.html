“Fuel for the Body, Peace for the Mind”

During a bike trip in the beautiful country of Myanmar, we stopped at a tiny village Market and were introduced to crackers made from local ingredients and spices. They were absolutely delicious, organic and pure. I set out to create my own version of these crackers and embody the notion that soul food really exists all over the world and revolves around these three simple ingredients: beans, rice and spices. The results are in this bag of SoFull Crackers. So full of passion, so full of creativity, so full of love and so full of favor.
